Step 1: Open Enterprise PM
When you open the NextGen Application Launcher, you will see a list of available platforms.

What to do: Locate and click on Enterprise PM (highlighted in the red circle below).

Why: This opens the Practice Management side of NextGen where patient scheduling, lookup, and demographics are handled.

Step 2: Open the Chart Lookup Window
Once Enterprise PM loads, you will see a blank gray screen with a blue icon toolbar across the top.

What to do: Look at the top toolbar and click the Chart icon (a clipboard with a magnifying glass, circled below).

Why: This triggers the "Patient Lookup" pop-up window to appear.

Step 3: Enter Patient Details
The Patient Lookup search window will pop up in the center of your screen.

What to do: Type the patient's information into the marked search fields:

Type the patient's last name in the Last box.

Type the patient's first name in the First / Preferred box.

Type their date of birth in the Birth Date box (Format: MM/DD/YYYY).

Action: Click the Find button at the bottom of the pop-up window.

Step 4: Select the Correct Patient
After clicking "Find", the system will display all matching results in the Matching Records box below your search criteria.

What to do: Verify that the name, Birth Date, and Address match your patient. Click on the correct patient's row to highlight it in blue (as shown with Ellis, Taylor).

Action: Click the Open button at the bottom right of the window.

Step 5: Navigating the Patient Chart
The patient's chart is now successfully loaded! You are automatically brought to the primary information view. To navigate different parts of their medical and account profile, use the four main lower tabs stretching across the middle of the screen:

Tab A: Demographics (Default View)
This is the view that opens first. It displays the patient's full address, primary care physician, age, telephone numbers, and preferred pharmacy details.

Tab B: Encounters
Click the Encounters tab on the far right of the sub-menu to view the patient's appointment and visit history. Here you can see the specific facility location (e.g., AdventHealth Blue Springs Surgery Center), rendering provider, and associated insurance.
