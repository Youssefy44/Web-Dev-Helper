⦁    HMO vs. PPO: Always ask the patient if their plan is HMO or PPO to locate it in the payer lookup.
⦁    BCBS Search: When searching for Blue Cross Blue Shield, type "BCBS" into the search field.
⦁    Dual Medicare: Enter as "Medicare complete".
⦁    Payer List Visibility: Stretch the payer list window to see the full insurance name. Verify using the FIN CLASS (found in the third column).
3. Policy, Group, & SSN Requirements
⦁    Missing Policy Number: If the patient cannot provide their policy number during the call:
⦁    Process the account as Self Pay.
⦁    Add the patient's SSN instead.
⦁    Enter the self-pay amount in the Mis800cellaneous Notes field.
⦁    Group Number: It is mandatory to ask for the group number. If the patient does not have it during the call, you may proceed without it.
4. Phone Numbers & Notes Entry
⦁    Customer Service Number: You must obtain the insurance customer service phone number and enter it into the Contact Phone field in Insurance Maintenance.
⦁    Note: If it does not auto-fill, ask the patient for the number.
⦁    General Insurance Notes: Enter any additional insurance-related notes into the Notes Box located in Insurance Maintenance.
